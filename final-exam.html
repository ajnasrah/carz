Question 4 of 10
What does "RWD" mean on a truck listing?

Correct Answer: Rear Wheel Drive - same as 2WD

RWD = 2WD = LOW DEMAND = COLD INVENTORY

Book values already adjust for 2WD vs 4WD. The real problem is DEMAND:

  • 2WD trucks sit on the lot 60-90 days vs 25-35 for 4WD
  • Longer time = more floor plan fees eating your profit
  • We target HIGH DEMAND (4WD) - don't stretch on cold inventory

Question 8 of 10
Floor plan fees accumulate over time. If a truck sits for 90 days instead of 30, how much EXTRA do you pay?

Correct Answer: $400

30 days = $200 floor plan
90 days = $600 floor plan
Extra cost = $400

This is why fast-selling configurations (4WD, crew cab) matter!

Question 9 of 10

Correct Answer: DO NOT BUY

Frame damage = structural integrity compromised = liability

Same goes for: FLOOD damage, SALVAGE title, FIRE damage

No discount makes these worth the risk.
